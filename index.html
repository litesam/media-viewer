Simple Steps to View Your Gallery

Accessing your local folders is easy. Just tell your browser where to look.

To view your images:
1. Open a new tab.
2. type file:/// followed by the full path to your folder. For example:
file:///C:/Users/YourName/Pictures/Vacation2025
3. Or drag and drop the folder into the browser address bar.

We support all standard web formats like PNG, JPG, GIF, BMP, and WebP. Our key feature is the built-in support for HEIC/HEIF, allowing you to view photos from modern smartphones without any extra software or cost.

For security reasons, browsers restrict websites from accessing your local file system. The `file:///` protocol is the standard, secure way to tell your browser to open and display a local file or directory. Our extension then enhances the view of that directory.
